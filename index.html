Welcome to Unicomm!

Unicomm is a website that allows students to interact with their professor seamlessly. It provides professors a site where they can easily place assignments, and extra materials for students to work on. Students can find solutions to exercises problem quickly and look at the recommended YouTube video on a certain topic(s). Additonally, the site provides a chat function where, using a bot, they can find solutions to the problem if it was asked by another student and the question were answered. The bot can also provide them with links to the exericse questions and solution and a youtube video. If the question has not been answered yet, the bot also connects students to anohter students suck on a similar question. And finally, the bot allows students to interact with their professor. The professor is provided with a digital whiteboard where they can work, step by step, through a problem with a student. Lastly the student could view their professor calender and find an open office hour to get a more in-depth response to their question.
